# PZAI Business App — Start Here

**Last Updated:** April 6, 2026

## What Is This?

The PZAI Business App is the iOS companion app for PZAI Systems clients. It lets business owners manage their website, view analytics, and communicate with Alex (our AI assistant) — all from their phone.

## Status: Pre-Development

We're collecting feature ideas and building the architecture. No Xcode project yet. When Mack is ready to start development, the codebase goes here.
