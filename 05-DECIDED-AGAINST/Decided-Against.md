# Decided Against

Decisions that were considered and explicitly rejected. Check here before suggesting a feature — it may have already been discussed and killed for good reason.

---

## Discord as Communication Channel (April 6, 2026)
**What:** Add Discord as a customer communication channel
**Why rejected:** Discord is for gaming/communities, not small business owners. A barbershop owner isn't on Discord. Would add complexity with near-zero adoption.

## General Public Marketing (April 6, 2026)
**What:** Run Facebook ads or general "we build websites" marketing to everyone
**Why rejected:** General web design is a commodity race to the bottom. PZAI only does niche marketing — targeting specific industries with industry-specific landing pages, pricing, and pain points. No general public ads. Ever.

## Three Visible Pricing Cards (April 6, 2026)
**What:** Show Free, Concierge ($49), and Growth ($99) as three side-by-side pricing options
**Why rejected:** Too many choices = paralysis. Customers see only Free vs Concierge. Growth is an upsell pop-up inside the Concierge checkout flow. Never show Growth on the main pricing page.

## Unlimited Design Customizations (April 6, 2026)
**What:** Let Concierge customers make unlimited design changes (colors, layout, fonts) forever
**Why rejected:** Design changes are expensive (manual work). Updates are free (automated). Solution: 7-day configuration window for unlimited design tweaks, then $5 per design change after that, or included on Growth. Prevents Frankensteining.
